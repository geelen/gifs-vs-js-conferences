A few types of talks

  • "Look at Spec X!"
  • "Look at this dumb hack!"

Speaking is costly

  • Time
  • Money
  • Stress

Time

  • Developing the proposal
  • Building the talk
  • Travel

Money

  • Time you're not earning
  • Rarely everything is covered
  • Reimbursements can be delayed

Stress

  • It's totally nerve-wrecking
  • You may not see/enjoy any of the other talks
  • Impostor syndrome right in the face

It's also pretty great

  • At least one person in the audience dug your talk and wants you to know it
  • The other speakers are awesome
  • You now know people in cool cities and can hang with them after

How to get accepted

  • Speak at MelbJS!
  • Think like the selection committee
  • Have something only you can talk about
  • Submit to literally everything

  • Diversity of everything
  • Local people

An idea!

  • A framework-agnostic core, with adapters for each framework
  • Consistent component API
  • Bare minimum of dependencies for each framework
  • One library, 4 entry points
